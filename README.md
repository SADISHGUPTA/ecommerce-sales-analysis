## 📌 Project Overview
In the highly competitive e-commerce space, understanding customer purchasing behavior and channel performance is critical for growth. This project serves as an end-to-end data analytics case study, transforming raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence. 

Through interactive visualization and advanced SQL querying, this repository demonstrates how to extract meaningful KPIs, identify revenue-driving segments, and monitor customer churn.

---

## 🛠️ Key Skills & Technologies Demonstrated

* **Relational Database Management (PostgreSQL):** * Wrote highly optimized queries to answer complex business questions.
  * Utilized **Common Table Expressions (CTEs)** and **Window Functions** (e.g., `LAG()`, `OVER()`) for time-series analysis and Year-over-Year (YoY) growth calculations.
  * Applied robust conditional logic and multi-table `JOIN` operations to segment user demographics and membership tiers.
* **Data Visualization & Dashboarding:** * Designed an interactive dashboard focusing on user experience and scannability.
  * Tracked and visualized core business KPIs including Total Revenue, Average Order Value (AOV), and Return Rates.
* **Business Acumen:** Translated raw technical outputs into strategic recommendations.

## 💡 Core Business Insights 

1. **Acquisition Channel Efficiency:** **Organic Search** is our highest-performing acquisition channel, driving the highest volume of *active* and engaged customers compared to paid channels.
2. **Mobile-First Revenue:** Device preferences skew heavily toward mobile platforms, which currently account for **55.6% of total revenue**. This indicates a strong need for mobile-optimized checkout flows to reduce friction.
3. **Retention & Churn:** Analyzed the revenue impact of single-time buyers versus repeated buyers, establishing a baseline to measure future loyalty programs.
